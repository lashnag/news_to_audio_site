Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with NewsToAudioBot? +
Just tap "Add to Telegram", enter /create_subscription and follow the quick setup. No email or registration required.
Can I customize which news I hear? +
Yes! Follow the setup instructions and add the channels you're interested in.
Is it free? +
Yes, the basic version includes one channel for free.
What if I don't like the voice? +
To use a unique voice, you need a 20-second audio recording as a voice sample.
